Why video is not loading?

  1. Because you have watched maximum number of videos.
  2. Because in your country maximum number of video reach.
  3. In your country video service is not available
  4. When screen initialize then few seconds before start task.

How to fix this problem

  1. Download free VPN App.
  2. Connect to USA server.
  3. Then restart Mastermind App.
  4. Wait for a minute, video will load successfully

Note

If video loading without VPN, Then use Mastermind App without VPN, If video is not loading then use VPN App.
